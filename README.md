# ChatGPT Impact Research Report

## Overview

This repository contains a **research and documentation report** that examines **ChatGPT**, a generative artificial intelligence model developed by **OpenAI**. The report explores the technology behind ChatGPT, its practical applications, benefits, limitations, and its growing influence across **Education, Information Technology, Healthcare, Cybersecurity, Software Development**, and other sectors.

The objective of this study is to provide a structured understanding of how **Conversational AI** is transforming **learning, productivity, communication, and decision-making** in modern society.

## Objectives

## Key Findings

The study highlights several important observations:

* **ChatGPT enhances access to information and educational resources.**
* **AI-powered assistants improve productivity and workflow efficiency.**
* **The technology supports learning, research, programming, content creation, and problem-solving.**
* **Ethical concerns such as misinformation, bias, privacy, and overreliance require careful consideration.**
* **Continued advancements in AI can improve accuracy, personalization, and domain-specific applications.**
